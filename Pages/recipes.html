Carbonara is a beloved Italian dish known for its rich and creamy texture. It features perfectly cooked pasta, usually spaghetti, tossed with a luxurious sauce made from eggs, grated cheese, pancetta or guanciale, and freshly ground black pepper. The heat from the pasta gently cooks the eggs, creating a velvety coating that clings to each strand. The savory pancetta adds a delightful crunch and a burst of flavor, while the cheese lends a touch of tanginess. Simple yet decadent, carbonara is a comforting classic that never fails to satisfy.
